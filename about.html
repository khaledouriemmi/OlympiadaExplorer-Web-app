About Us

Welcome to Olympic Explorer! This website is created with the intention of promoting the Olympics and is a project aimed at developing tech skills. We want to clarify that this website has no commercial intentions and does not intend to violate any copyright laws.

This project began as a school project for a high school Olympiad but has since grown into a more serious endeavor. Our goal is to provide valuable information about the Olympics and help others learn and explore the event.

Please note that we do not store any data about our users. Your privacy is important to us, and we are committed to ensuring that your information remains secure.

Thank you for visiting our site. We hope you find it informative and engaging as we continue to learn and develop our skills.
